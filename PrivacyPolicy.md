# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 15, 2026

Lucky Boba ("we", "us", "our") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the **Lucky Boba POS** mobile application (the "App").

## 1. Information We Collect
When you use the App, we may collect the following types of information:
*   **Personal Information:** Your name, email address, and profile photo when you sign in using Google Authentication (Firebase Auth).
*   **Device Information:** We collect crash logs and diagnostic data to improve app stability using Firebase Crashlytics.
*   **Usage Data:** Logs of items added to your cart, branch selections, and order history.

## 2. Permissions We Request
*   **Camera:** The app may request camera access to scan QR codes for promo redemptions or payments.
*   **Location:** We request location access to find the nearest Lucky Boba branch to you.
*   **Storage / Photos:** To cache images (like menu item photos) and reduce your data usage.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
*   To create and manage your account.
*   To process your orders and link them to the correct point-of-sale branch.
*   To communicate with you regarding your order status.
*   To improve our App’s performance through analytics and crash reporting.

## 4. Third-Party Services
We use trusted third parties to provide essential infrastructure:
*   **Google (Firebase):** We use Google Firebase for Google Sign-In, database hosting, and Crashlytics.
*   **Hostinger / Supabase:** Our primary POS database runs securely on these servers.
*   **Stripe:** (If applicable) Used securely to process digital payments. We do not store your raw credit card numbers.

## 5. Data Deletion
You can request the permanent deletion of your account and associated data by:
1. Opening the Profile tab in the App.
2. Clicking "Delete Account".
3. Or by contacting our support team.
